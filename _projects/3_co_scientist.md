An agent for autonomous scientific workflows: deep literature review, hypothesis generation and critique, experiment planning with explicit baselines, automated execution and tracking, and technical-report drafting with citations.

Compresses literature surveys from **1–2 weeks to 1–2 days** and full research iteration cycles from **~3 months to ~1 month**.

The design choice that mattered: forcing the agent to write a baseline plan before any code runs is what makes its experiments interpretable. Without that gate, autonomous agents tend to produce numbers that nobody can stand behind. With it, the human reviews the plan once, then trusts the execution.
