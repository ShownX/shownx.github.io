GPU-accelerated Fully Homomorphic Encryption (CKKS, BFV) achieving **~45× speedup at zero accuracy loss**, enabling encrypted inference and similarity search for regulated markets such as financial services and healthcare.

The contribution most academic FHE work skips: making the polynomial arithmetic memory-bandwidth efficient on real GPUs, not just asymptotically faster. End-to-end speedup at deployable batch sizes is the metric that decides whether FHE is a research curiosity or a product feature.
